Adams Industries is a Baton Rouge–based construction firm, family-run, now in its third decade. We cover the full arc of a project — design, management, and the skilled construction labor that actually puts work in place. Commercial, residential, industrial, and marine. One point of contact from the first meeting through the last inspection.

## What Sets Us Apart

The Louisiana market is full of two kinds of contractors: the big institutional firms with polished websites and regional managers in other states, and the outfits that show up after a storm and disappear when the next one hits. Both of them have their place, but neither of them is what we are.

Adams Industries is deliberately positioned between the two. Large enough to pull permits in any parish, run multi-property recoveries, and carry the right insurance. Small enough that the owner answers the phone and walks the site personally. Twenty-plus years of the same name on the paperwork means we're the contractor that's still here a year later when a warranty item shows up — and every year before that, too.

## What We Do

- **Four sectors.** Residential, commercial, industrial, and marine. Most Louisiana contractors pick one. We take the call either way.
- **Full scope.** Design, management, and skilled trades. One crew, one schedule, one paper trail.
- **Louisiana-specific.** We know the parishes, the permitting quirks, the carriers, and the storms. Twenty years of the small stuff that only matters when it matters.
- **Storm response.** Set up to mobilize in hours, not days, when the Gulf sends something.
- **Direct access.** The principal is on the phone number at the top of every page. No triage.
